Serving and Protecting Our Community

About Us

The Longhill City Police Department is dedicated to maintaining public safety and enhancing the quality of life for all residents. Our officers are committed to upholding the law with integrity and respect, fostering a safe and secure environment for everyone.

Services

  • Emergency Response
  • Crime Prevention
  • Community Policing
  • Traffic Enforcement
  • Investigations
  • Public Safety Education

Departments

Training & Discipline Unit

Our Training & Discipline Unit ensures that all officers are well-prepared and adhere to the highest standards of conduct.

Academy Security Unit

The Academy Security Unit is dedicated to maintaining the safety and security of our police academy and its trainees.

Traffic Unit

Our Traffic Unit is responsible for ensuring the safe and efficient flow of traffic throughout the city, as well as enforcing traffic laws.

SWAT Unit

Our SWAT Unit is trained to handle high-risk operations and critical incidents, ensuring the safety of our community.

Police Headquarters

The Police Headquarters is the central hub for all administrative and operational activities of the department.

Police Unit

The Police Unit is responsible for general law enforcement duties, including patrolling neighborhoods, responding to emergencies, and investigating crimes.
